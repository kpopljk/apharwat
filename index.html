    The Origin Debate

    Why Kashmiri Walnuts?

    Not all walnuts are created equal. While imported varieties focus on appearance, Kashmiri walnuts are prized for their superior nutritional profile and rich, natural oils.

    "Superior in taste, organic by nature, and nutrient-dense by origin."
    The Gold Standard

    Apharwat Kashmiri

    • High Oil Content: Rich, creamy texture naturally occurring in Himalayan soil.
    • Organic Growth: 100% natural farming without synthetic pesticides.
    • Omega-3 Rich: Higher density of brain-boosting fatty acids.
    Mass Market

    California / Imported

    • Low Oil Content: Typically dry, brittle, and lacks depth of flavor.
    • Machine Processed: Often involves high-heat drying and bleaching.

    From Peak to Pack

    The Walnut Processing Journey

    Every Apharwat walnut passes through rigorous quality checkpoints to ensure premium purity and freshness.

    1

    Harvesting

    Expert farmers harvest walnut fruits from high-altitude orchards during peak season using traditional methods.

    2

    Cleaning & Sorting

    Freshly harvested walnuts are carefully cleaned, sorted by color and size grades (Extra Light, Light Amber, Halves, Quarters).

    3

    Precision Drying

    A GMP-compliant Hot Air Dryer maintains strict temperature control (35-40C) to reduce moisture below 5% without compromising oil integrity.

    4

    Vacuum Sealing

    Precisely weighed portions are filled with oxygen absorbers and vacuum-sealed to lock in freshness for up to 12 months.

    Walnuts in Your Kitchen

    TraditionalSpicy

    Kashmiri Doon Chetin

    A traditional Kashmiri walnut chutney blending chopped walnuts with fresh green chilies, ginger, and creamy yogurt. The oils from Kashmiri walnuts create an exceptionally rich flavor. Serve with bread or as a condiment.

    Key Ingredients:

    • 250g Apharwat walnuts
    • 3 green chilies
    • 1 cup yogurt
    2
    DessertSweet

    Walnut Baklava

    Delicate layers of crispy phyllo pastry layered with crushed Apharwat walnuts, cinnamon, and drizzled with fragrant honey syrup. The high oil content of Kashmiri walnuts ensures a luxuriously moist baklava that stays fresh for days.

    Key Ingredients:

    • 500g phyllo pastry
    • 400g Apharwat walnuts
    • 100g ghee
