The Walnut Processing Journey

From tree to vacuum seal, we ensure perfection.

1

Harvesting

Expert farmers harvest walnut fruits from high-altitude orchards during peak season.

2

Cleaning & Sorting

Freshly harvested walnuts are carefully cleaned and sorted by color and size grades.

3

Precision Drying

A GMP-compliant Hot Air Dryer maintains strict temperature control (35-40°C).

4

Vacuum Sealing

Precisely weighed portions are filled with oxygen absorbers and vacuum-sealed.
